Rigoletto Cafe

About

Cafe Rigoletto is close to the Palazzo Ducale a historical palace
full of history and nature. In this cafe, you can test one of the best Marocchino coffee which is the speciality of the house.
There aren't much-sitting spaces inside but on a sunny day, you can sit outside and look at the amazing surrounding space full of history and shops.
